Frequently asked questions

Can I change plans at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the remaining balance will be credited to your account.
What happens if I exceed my limits?
We'll notify you when you're approaching your limits. If you exceed them, your services will continue running and we'll reach out to discuss upgrading your plan. We never surprise you with unexpected charges.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! The Pro plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll have full access to all Pro features during the trial period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), as well as PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice with NET-30 terms.
